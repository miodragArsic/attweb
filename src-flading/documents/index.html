Time saving

Smartliving is your personal assitant making decisions for you and save you precious time. Whether it is waking you up on time, controlling your heating system automatically or watering your plants. You don’t have to bother anymore about these tasks.

Energy saving

Smartliving can turn off appliances in and around your home when you are not there. The Heating turns on automatically when you approach your home and turns it off when the last person has left the home.

More comfort

We bring connected solutions to the masses and aims to improve our quality of life by making things smarter. We allow them to talk to each other and make anonymous decisions.
